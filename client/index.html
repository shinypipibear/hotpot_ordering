Chongqing hot pot is also known as spicy hot pot. At the beginning, the paddler who worked on the wharf cut ox tripe and liver into strips, and cooked them in a iron basin which has been divided into parts with different soup base on a clay stove. Because of the low price and delicious taste, it attracted lots of labor workers.

Now it has become popular around the world. The ingredients also becomes varied. There are both spicy and non-spicy soup base for you to choose.
